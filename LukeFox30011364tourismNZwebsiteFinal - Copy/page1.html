Destination Ferry Ride

Destination: Wellington

The capital of NZ. The CBD is known for being a party town

with universities around the city and pubs inbetween

Ask a person from Wellington if it's windy, and they will probably say: "not really"

... it's a windy place though!

Very diverse place and people. Lots of friendly characters in 'Welly'.
